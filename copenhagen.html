Info

We will travel to Copenhagen, Denmark with a group of 30 students & teachers. We will visit several companies as well as a university. The trip also gives you the opportunity to explore the city by day and night, includes cultural activities and a visit to Malmö!

When?

The studytrip will take place from the 17th till the 26th of April, which includes 2 days of travelling by train. This gives us plenty of time to explore the beautiful city of Denmark.

Travel

As a means to reduce costs and help saving the planet, we will be taking the train instead of a plane to Copenhagen! This trip will take us 11 hours, but it means you can take a whole lot more luggage on this trip. You also have a lot more space to stretch your legs and walk around.

How much?

The price of this trip will be around €250. The exact price can still be a bit lower, but this is based on the amount of participants and acquisition.

You can always contact us via studytrip@svsticky.nl

Schedule

The following schedule informs you about sign-up time and compulsory activities for participants right up to the departure date.

  • November 17th

    Sign-ups close

    You have until November 17th to sign up for the studytrip. After this date we'll select the definitive participants with a weighed lottery based on motivation, CV, education level and participation.

  • November

    Participants meeting

    This meeting everyone will receive the necessary details for the trip. All participants will be required to sign a participation contract. The meeting will be November 27th, and will start at 5:30PM

  • December

    Payment due

    You will have to pay off the trip by december 31st. This is plenty of time to up your DUO if you have to ;)

  • Januari - June

    Sponsor events

    There will be one day filled with company visits/lectures. This days will be disclosed shortly. Attendence is mandatory.

  • April 17th

    Departure

    We will depart for Copenhagen at 07:07 from Utrecht Centraal.
